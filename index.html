About Me

I am a Telecommunications Graduate Engineer specialising in network design and infrastructure. I have hands-on experience in designing and implementing network solutions, monitoring performance, managing security protocols, and configuring devices such as Sophos and Cisco routers, switches, and firewalls.

I offer a broad range of ICT services, including CCTV installation, website development, home and SME network consultation and installations and tech project prototyping. I am proficient in Python, C++, HTML, JavaScript and CSS, and I work comfortably with tools such as Visual Studio Code, Proteus, EasyEDA, Cisco Packet Tracer, Advanced Design System (ADS), and Arduino IDE.

My work is driven by a passion for creating practical, impactful solutions. I strive to leverage technology to improve connectivity and accessibility, while continually growing and contributing meaningful innovations.
